Record
Have the application listening and recording whenever someone speaks!

Steps:

  • Enter your EchoKit server's WebSocket URL, such as ws://34.44.85.57:9090/ws/
  • Select and turn on "Recording mode"
  • Click on "Connect"
  • Click on "Start Listening"
  • Start speaking!
