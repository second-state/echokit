Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my EchoKit?

After you upload your materials (1 day) and we train your custom AI (2 days), we ship worldwide. Most customers receive their EchoKit within 7-10 days of ordering.

Do I need to be technical to set this up?

Not at all! Our Creator Studio guides you through each step. Just upload audio files, type your stories, and we handle the rest. Setup takes about 30 minutes.

What's the difference between Starter and Advanced?

Starter ($99) uses pre-built AI voice styles you can customize. Advanced ($149) clones an actual voice from just 3 minutes of audio—perfect for preserving a loved one's voice.

Can I update the content later?

Yes! Advanced Edition includes ongoing content updates, so you can add new stories, recipes, or memories anytime.

Does EchoKit need WiFi?

Yes, EchoKit connects to your home WiFi for voice processing. It works anywhere within WiFi range and doesn't require a computer or phone.

Can I get a refund?

Each EchoKit is custom-trained with your voice and content, so we cannot offer refunds once training begins. However, we'll work with you to ensure you're satisfied with the result.
